Hostel Management System (HMS)

Why HMS?

                     A big question to those who like it in legacy way (old paper based).

                     And answer to this, if we want conclude in one sentence, then it can be

                     ‘To save our most valuable entity “time” ultimately. ‘



But on other hand if we elaborate this we come up with:

        365x24x7 Official work support (boon to those Saturday /Sunday haters.)

Ø  Free from all that paper and form hassle.

Ø  Have to apply for hostel? do it online on HMS, we made it convenient.

Ø  Are you waiting for Allotment procedure to finish and allotted list? 

Don’t worry, now HMS is there it will notify you on your HMS profile if you are allotted any seat (it’s easy no need to visit offices to check list).

Ø  Have to pay hostel fees? its super easy do it online with your Card (Rest your body because now no more hassle of going to bank for fee payment).

Ø  Simple UI makes it super easy to use (makes it convenient for students to interact to hostel authorities).

Ø  No ques, no waiting (rest your legs and peace your mind because, No more walks to offices).

Ø  Got complaints but stucked somewhere? no problem, do it on the go from your HMS profile (for hostlers).



“And that’s how we saved your time and efforts by cutting down chunks of time from various official and non-official tasks.”




How to do it in right way?

 

Ø  For new hostel applicants:

1.        Go to HMS homepage and go to Student registration link

2.       Fill out the required details. (like Addhar Number as your username, name mobile, etc.)

3.        Now click Register to save and register.

Your done here it’s that simple.

ü  things to remember “if you get any kind of error while registering (except primary key delicacy, in case of this error recheck your Addhar because its already registered with HMS causing this error) contact technical helpdesk.”

4.       Now login with your login credentials and fill rest of details.

5.       After this fill out and save hostel preferences.

6.       Now After This Go to Application form section and Check full details in form and Click ‘I Agree Button’ (This is very important because without pressing this button registration will be canceled automatically after last date of agreeing)

7.       After this take a print out of this application form and submit it to Chief Warden office with a copy of your University ID Card for verification of form.

8.       And here your part ends. Now wait and check your form verification status, if your form gets verified by admin then wait for allotment process to complete and if you are allotted any seat you will be notified on you HMS profile. (But if your form does not get verified and you think you was eligible then contact administration personally via mail or call or even face to face if required).

9.       If you are allotted a hostel, then pay given amount of fee for seat reserving in given time period or after given time limit your allotment will be canceled.

10.     After successful payment and payment confirmation on HMS you profile gets another option to choose seat in hostel where it shows all available seats and you can choose any available seat.

Here this process ends.

 

Ø  For current hostlers:

For those who lives in hostel HMS may seem to have less use, but it’s not like what it seems to be it’s an important tool for hostlers as:

1.        It provides you On the go facility to register any complaint and also check whether your request or complaint has been considered or not.

2.       Reminds you to pay fee on time.

3.        Get your warden and care-taker details on the go no need to worry if your warden or care-taker changes.

And that’s how we go
